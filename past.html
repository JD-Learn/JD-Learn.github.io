Past Pursuits

I worked as a print production artist for about ten years, although I enjoyed and still enjoy graphic design to an extent I now prefer working on personal projects as a form of creative expression and personal therapy rather than meeting impossible deadlines.

My last year as a profession production artist, I would spend moments at my desk just staring outside the window because for me the work was no longer fulfilling and more and more my creativity waned and waned. I supposed the view outside my window had a hand in making me want to pursue another career and impact people in different ways.

Although I am no longer professionally a part of the creative world, I am still an art enthusiast. Currently, I like discovering murals that instantly transform mundanity and cold, concrete walls into an artist's canvas.
